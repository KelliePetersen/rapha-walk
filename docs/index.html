WHAT IS RAPHA WALK?

The Rapha Walk is a yearly fundraiser for Rapha Clinic, organised by the Emerging Healthcare Leaders student group of UWG.

The Rapha Walk is a 5 kilometer (3.1 miles) track along Carrollton's Green Belt. It begins at Hobbs Farm and loops around the surrounding area.

LEARN MORE

EVENT OVERVIEW

  • DATE Sunday April 11th, 2020
  • LOCATION Hobbs Farm Disc Golf Course
  • DISTANCE 5 kilometers (3.1 miles)
  • SCHEDULE 11am - 3pm
  • COST $20 ($15 before April 1st)

RAPHA CLINIC

All proceeds from the event go to the Rapha Clinic of West Georgia.

Rapha Clinic provides medical and dental services to members of our community in need, at little to no cost to them. If you'd like to learn more about Rapha Clinic, visit their website.

HOW TO PARTICIPATE

Participating in the Rapha Walk is simple. When you register for the event, your name is added to a participants list. On the day of the Walk, make your way to Hobbs Farm by 11am, and let our volunteers check your name off the list.
