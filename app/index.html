WHAT IS RAPHA WALK?

The Rapha Walk is a yearly fundraiser for Rapha Clinic, organised by the Emerging Healthcare Leaders student group of UWG.

The Rapha Walk is a 5 kilometer (3.1 miles) track along Carrollton's Green Belt. It begins at Hobbs Farm and loops around the surrounding area.
